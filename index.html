Bluetooth RC Car – Android + Arduino

Compose app + HC‑05 + L293D (Uno)

Android App

Open android in Android Studio (Giraffe+). Build and run. Requires Android 7.0+.

Arduino Firmware

Open arduino/rc_car.ino in Arduino IDE 2.3.2+. Upload to Uno. HC‑05 at 9600 baud.

Wiring

  • HC‑05 TX → Pin 10, RX ← Pin 11
  • M1 Steering: IN1=2, IN2=3, EN1(PWM)=5
  • M3 Propulsion: IN3=4, IN4=6, EN3(PWM)=7
